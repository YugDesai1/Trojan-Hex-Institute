Since it's establishment in 1955, Trojen Hex instituion has been home to some of the greatest minds of the world and has been contributing tremendously in the field of education. At Trojen Hex institute students not only study but also gain practical knowledge in their desired topic of interests. Not us but the student themselves have raised the standards of this instituion by excelling in each and every aspects of life. May it be your skills or may it be your knowledge at Trojen Hex we have got you covered.Our instituion has been awarded with some of the most prestigious awards since the year 1965 to till date. Some of out most remarkable achievements are "The Desai Academic Award (1980)","Gaur Sahab Innovation Award (1991)","Thakur Devi Utkarshtata Puraskaar (1995)","Siddharth Sammaan Puraskaar (2008)" and many more. We can proudly say no matter the situation Trojen Hex Institute will come through!. We welcome you to join us in this majestic experience and make your life a blissful one.
